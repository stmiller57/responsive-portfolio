About Stephen

Stephen Miller graduated from Saint Joseph's University with a B.A. in English. He spent the first 20 years of his professional life as a sportswriter, covering everything from the Phillies to college basketball to high school football.

Stephen is currently studying to switch careers and become a web developer. In his spare time, he loves to watch sports, read, listen to music, and spend time with his wife and two boys.
