About Stephen

Stephen Miller is a veteran writer and editor who is currently studying web development. He spent the first 20 years of his professional life as a sportswriter, covering everything from the Phillies to college basketball to high school football. He has never worked for President Trump.

Stephen owns a B.A. in English from Saint Joseph's University, where he concentrated his studies in writing. He graduated with honors from the school's honors program.

In his spare time, Stephen loves to watch sports, read, listen to music, and spend time with his wife and two boys. He somehow ended up a fan of the Philadelphia Eagles, New York Mets and Boston Celtics. Ask him if you want the whole story.
